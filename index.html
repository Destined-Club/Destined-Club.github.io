THE MOST FULFILLING TRAVEL EXPERIENCE

You want to have the best, most fulfilling travel experience. But there is too much information and it is not really catered to your liking. So you feel confused and don’t know where to go and what to do. The information exists but finding it is just too complicated and it is for an average experience for everyone. But you want something exactly for you.  

We have been there too. Frustrated and afraid to make another silly mistake in a new place full of opportunities.

We have combined the recommendations of your favorite tastemakers, trailblazers, and connoisseurs in the simplest and in the most beautiful possible form. A list of places of where to eat, where to run, where to chill, where to have the best coffee and pastry. Curated by the ones, who you know will cater to your life and your style. To unlock the best of your desired destination.

EMILIA VAHVELAINEN, BAJA CALIFORNIA

Hotel San Cristobal: Heaven on Earth at the End of a Dirt Road

Details details details...Nestled between the Sierra de la Laguna mountains and the Pacific Ocean, this has to be the most picturesque hotel on earth. Close to a charming fishing village of Todos Santos,  at the end of a dirt road, San Cristobal is a true love letter to Mexican aesthetics.

EVELINA ROMANO, PARIS

Officine Universelle Buly 1803: A Modern Apothecary for Thoughtfull Gifts

Candles, scents, beautiful soaps with your own initials....this is the place for the most beautiful, most wonderful, most detailed personalised gifts. Gift your BFF with a lip balm with their initials and select the colour of the leather of the gift box yourself.

ANNA VAININEN, NEW YORK

Bar Orai: A Hidden Vinyl-Listening Japanese Whiskey Speakeasy

Bar Orai, this vinyl-listening Japanese whiskey speakeasy is hidden inside of an NYC apartment. There is no website advertising the bar, only an Instagram account! Such a cool place to visit 🥃
