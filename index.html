You want to have the best, most fulfilling travel experience. But there is too much information and it is not really catered to your liking. So you feel confused and don’t know where to go and what to do. The information exists but finding it is just too complicated and it is for an average experience for everyone. But you want something exactly for you.  

We have been there too. Frustrated and afraid to make another silly mistake in a new place full of opportunities.

We have combined the recommendations of your favorite tastemakers, trailblazers, and connoisseurs in the simplest and in the most beautiful possible form. A list of places of where to eat, where to run, where to chill, where to have the best coffee and pastry. Curated by the ones, who you know will cater to your life and your style. To unlock the best of your desired destination.
