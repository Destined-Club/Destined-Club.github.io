Destined App Support

Welcome to the support page for the Destined App. Here, you'll find answers to common questions and information to help you make the most of our app.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is the Destined App?

The Destined App offers curated travel itineraries, providing recommendations from trusted individuals to enhance your travel experiences.

How can I download the Destined App?

The Destined App is available for download on the Apple App Store. Please note that it is currently only available in Finland.

What is the cost of the subscription?

The subscription fee for the Destined App is €34.99 per year.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

You can manage or cancel your subscription through your Apple ID account settings on your iPhone. For detailed instructions, please refer to Apple's support page on how to manage subscriptions.

How is my personal data handled?

We are committed to protecting your privacy. For detailed information on how we collect, use, and protect your personal data, please read our Privacy Policy.

What are the terms of use for the Destined App?

By using the Destined App, you agree to our terms of use. Please review them here: Terms of Use.
